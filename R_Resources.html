RStudio Basics

Purpose

What is R?

R is a programming language and environment for statistical computing and graphics. This means that R can be used to do a variety of statistical analyses. You can also use R to graphically represent your data and manipulate your data.

Why learn how to use R?

It is free and widely used in the life sciences (as well as in other fields such as Data Science)

It can be much faster and more streamlined than using other tools such as Excel and GraphPad

It allows for more reproducible analyses

It is much more flexible than other tools

It is open-sourced, people are constantly producing new tools and improving on old ones

Nonetheless, learning R can be time-consuming and overwhelming. Below are some helpful tutorials and resources.
